Welcome & Introduce

Hello! My name is Sasa Katsuhiro!

I am a skillful and highly organized engineer with BEng (Hons) in the Electronic Engineering awarded by Nottingham Trent University (NTU) in 2022 and currently a PhD student in the field of Medical Technology based at Medical Technologies Innovation Facility (MTIF) funded by NTU from October 2022 after skipping a master's degree. I have over 10 years of business experience before becoming a student and worked in four different countries, including the UK.

My business experience includes software development to automate the database operations, in addition to my previous experience as an infrastructure engineer designing, building, operating and maintaining servers/network infrastructure.

In the lectures, labs and projects at NTU, I have gained a broad range of engineering skills and insight into a field of computer vision through research in the object detection and the image classification using machine learning in the summer placement and the final year project, respectively, in addition to the technical competencies and knowledges required in the latest engineering fields such as IoT devices, sensors, and VLSI / FPGAs.

Coding

As an infrastructure engineer, I’ve developed many tools and software to automate the routine jobs and to proactively detect sings of system and network failure in C, bash, zsh, vbs, and bat. I developed a practical object detector by webcam inference using a machine learning techniques for 10 electronic components such as resistor and capacitor. The model is trained with 2000+ images on Convolutional Neural Network, and the Single Shot Multibox Detection method is employed for a realtime detection.

TCP/IP Network

Servers

From the blade server systems to the small single-board computers such as Raspberry Pi, I have an extensive knowledge to design and configure scalable server systems for the web, mail, VoIP, proxy, DNS, and authentication services in the mixed-OS environment with Windows, Red Hat, and Cent OS operating system. Also, not only for the physical systems, I have a practical knowledge and experience to migrate physical server systems to the virtual environment using VMware products.
